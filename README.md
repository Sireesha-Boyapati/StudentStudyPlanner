Student Study Planner

Project Overview

Student Study Planner is a web-based productivity platform designed to help university students organize their studies, manage assignments, track progress, and generate personalized study schedules.

The system enables students to:

- Manage subjects and study topics
- Track assignments and deadlines
- Create study schedules
- Monitor study progress
- View productivity analytics

Problem Statement

Many students struggle with:

- Poor time management
- Missed deadlines
- Lack of study planning
- Difficulty prioritizing tasks

This platform helps students improve productivity through effective planning and progress tracking.
